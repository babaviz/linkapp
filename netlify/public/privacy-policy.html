🔒 Privacy Policy

Version 1.0.0 | Last Updated: December 28, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use LinkApp.

1. Introduction

Welcome to LinkApp ("we," "our," or "us"). By using LinkApp, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use LinkApp, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password, and profile picture
  • Profile Information: Bio, preferences, location, and other details you choose to share
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed securely through Paystack (we do not store your full payment card details)
  • Property Listings: Information about properties you list, including descriptions, images, location, and pricing
  • Communications: Messages, chat conversations, and other communications through the app

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: GPS location to show nearby properties and services
  • Usage Data: App features you use, pages viewed, time spent
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable property listings, searches, and transactions
  • Facilitate service bookings and job applications
  • Process payments and referral rewards
  • Enable messaging, chat, and video calling features

3.2 Personalization

  • Customize content based on your location and preferences
  • Provide personalized recommendations for properties and services
  • Show nearby properties and services using location data

3.3 Safety and Security

  • Verify user identities and prevent fraud
  • Monitor for suspicious activity and policy violations
  • Protect against security threats and abuse
  • Moderate content for safety and compliance

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate and improve our services, including:

  • Authentication and analytics services: For secure login and app performance monitoring
  • Database and cloud hosting providers: For secure data storage and backend infrastructure
  • Payment processors: For secure payment processing and transaction management (we do not store your full payment card details)
  • Communication services: For chat, messaging, and video calling features
  • Mapping services: For location-based features and property mapping
  • App distribution platforms: For app updates and maintenance

4.2 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal processes, government requests, or to prevent fraud and protect user safety.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of passwords and sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-compliant providers

5.2 Data Retention

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Transaction records: As required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Communications: According to our data retention policy

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and Portability: Request a copy of your personal information

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your information

  • Do Not Sell: We do not sell your personal or sensitive information to third parties, advertisers, or data brokers

7. Location Data

LinkApp uses location services to show properties and services near you. You can control location permissions through your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Important: LinkApp is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Push Notifications

We use push notification services to send you important updates about messages, property inquiries, service bookings, and more. You can disable notifications in your device settings or app preferences at any time.

10. Important Notice

We do not sell your personal or sensitive information. Your privacy is important to us. We only share your information with trusted service providers as described in this policy to deliver and improve our services.
