🛡️ Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority

⚠️ In Case of Emergency

If you feel unsafe or threatened, contact local emergency services immediately.

Nigeria Emergency: 112 or 767

Important: These safety guidelines are designed to help you use LinkApp safely. Please read them carefully and follow them when interacting with other users.

1. General Safety Tips

1.1 Protect Your Personal Information

  • Never share: Full address, financial information, passwords, or sensitive documents early in communication
  • Be cautious with: Phone numbers, workplace details, and daily routines
  • Use the app: Keep conversations within LinkApp's messaging system initially
  • Verify identities: Be skeptical of profiles that seem too good to be true

1.2 Trust Your Instincts

Warning Signs:

  • Requests for money or financial assistance
  • Pressure to move conversations off the platform quickly
  • Inconsistent stories or information
  • Reluctance to meet in person or video call
  • Aggressive or inappropriate behavior

2. Meeting in Person

2.1 First Meetings

  • Meet in public: Choose busy, well-lit public places (cafes, malls, restaurants)
  • Inform someone: Tell a friend or family member where you're going and who you're meeting
  • Arrange your own transport: Don't rely on your date for transportation
  • Stay sober: Keep your wits about you and don't leave drinks unattended
  • Have an exit plan: Ensure you can leave safely if needed

2.2 Property Viewings

  • Bring someone: Never view properties alone, especially in isolated areas
  • Schedule wisely: View properties during daylight hours
  • Verify listings: Confirm property details and ownership before viewing
  • Meet agents publicly first: If possible, meet the agent in a public place before viewing
  • Share your location: Use location-sharing features with trusted contacts

3. Online Safety

3.1 Account Security

  • Use a strong, unique password for LinkApp
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available
  • Don't share your login credentials with anyone
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Report suspicious login attempts

3.2 Avoiding Scams

Common Scams to Watch Out For:

  • Advance Fee Fraud: Requests for payment before services or property viewing
  • Fake Listings: Properties that don't exist or aren't actually for rent/sale
  • Romance Scams: Building fake relationships to request money
  • Job Scams: Fake job offers requiring upfront payment
  • Phishing: Fake emails or messages asking for login details

4. DateMi Safety (Social Connections)

4.1 Before Meeting

  • Get to know the person through messages and video calls first
  • Verify their identity through video calls (not just photos)
  • Share your plans with friends or family
  • Research the person online if possible

4.2 During Dates

  • Meet in public places for the first several dates
  • Don't feel pressured to go anywhere you're uncomfortable
  • Have your own transportation arranged
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible
  • Trust your instincts - leave if you feel unsafe

5. Financial Safety

5.1 Payment Protection

  • Use secure methods: Only pay through verified, secure payment platforms
  • Avoid cash payments: Especially for large transactions or to strangers
  • Get receipts: Always obtain documentation for payments made
  • Verify before paying: Confirm service provider credentials and reviews

5.2 Red Flags

Never send money for:

  • Emergency requests from people you haven't met
  • Property deposits without viewing in person
  • Job opportunities requiring upfront payment
  • Investments or opportunities that seem too good to be true

6. Reporting and Blocking

6.1 How to Report

Report users who:

  • Harass, threaten, or abuse you
  • Post fake or fraudulent listings
  • Request money inappropriately
  • Violate community guidelines
  • Engage in suspicious or illegal activities

6.2 How to Block

You can block users at any time if you feel uncomfortable. Blocked users cannot:

  • Send you messages
  • View your profile
  • See your listings
  • Contact you through the app

7. Privacy Settings

  • Review and adjust your privacy settings regularly
  • Control who can see your location
  • Manage profile visibility settings
  • Control message permissions

8. For Parents and Guardians

Age Restriction: LinkApp is only for users 18 years and older. If you discover a minor using the app, please report it immediately.

9. Additional Resources

  • Nigeria Police Force: 112 or 767
  • Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC): 0809-932-2222
  • LinkApp Support: support@link-app.co

10. Remember

Your safety is in your hands:

  • Take your time getting to know people
  • Don't let anyone pressure you into doing anything uncomfortable
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, it probably is
  • We're here to help - contact support if you have concerns
